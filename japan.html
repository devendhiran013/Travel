Travel Tips

Transportation:

Japan has an extensive and efficient public transportation system. Consider getting a Japan Rail Pass for unlimited travel on JR trains. Familiarize yourself with subway and train systems in major cities like Tokyo and Osaka.

Cultural Etiquette:

Be respectful of Japanese customs and traditions. For example, it's customary to bow slightly when greeting someone. Remove your shoes when entering someone's home or certain traditional accommodations like ryokans.
